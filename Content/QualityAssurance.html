Quality Control

In order to control the quality of the project, the development team will review what has been done at the end of each sprint. The quality of the product will be compared with the requirements. If discrepancies are found they will be discussed amongst the team in order to find a resolution. Development of the product will be following the list of acceptance tests in order to meet all acceptance criteria.For more information please check the Quality Assurance plan.
